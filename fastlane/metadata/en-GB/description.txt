Farm Survivorr is an survivor defence game where you collect a group of characters to protect your farm base from enemies. Your goal is to strategise, beat waves, upgrade your heroes and survive all of the seasons while keeping your base safe!

You begin your quest with staring characters, after playing a game for a bit you will earn enough ingame currency to expand your group with more characters. With your group you need to defend the base for ten waves, fifth wave for elite group of enemies and tenth is a boss wave. Each subsequent level becomes harder, so its important to upgrade and build your group in a strategic and smart way. 

Upgrade your heres, raise their stats, increase total healthpool, pump the attack damage and make them more agile increasing attack speed. Alternativelly buy bombs and hearts to damage enemies and heal allies as a commander or even better buy new characters!
